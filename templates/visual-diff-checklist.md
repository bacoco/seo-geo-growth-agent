## Checks

- No horizontal overflow.
- No clipped title or card text.
- URLs and code wrap correctly.
- Tables remain readable.
- Screenshot images load or show a clear missing-file state.
- P0/P1/P2 hierarchy is visible above the fold.
- The title is short: `Audit SEO/GEO — [site]`.

## Verdict

- Pass
- Needs design fix
- Needs content reduction
- Needs responsive fix
