# 07 — Local SEO Addendum

Use this when “GEO” means geographic/local SEO, or when the business has physical locations or service areas.

## Local SEO signals

| Area | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Google Business Profile | Claimed, verified, accurate categories, services, hours, photos |
| NAP consistency | Name, address, phone consistent across site and directories |
| Location pages | Unique content per location, not thin doorway duplicates |
| Service-area pages | Real service coverage, proof, testimonials, FAQs |
| Reviews | Ethical review generation and response workflow |
| Local schema | LocalBusiness, address, geo, openingHours, areaServed |
| Local links | Chambers, associations, local partners, events, sponsorships |
| Local content | City-specific proof, logistics, regulations, pricing context |

## LocalBusiness schema reminder

Use LocalBusiness schema only when the business has a real local presence or service area. Keep NAP consistent with the visible page and Google Business Profile.
