# Earned Media and Entity Strategy

AI answer engines often synthesize information from more than the brand’s own site. Strengthen authentic entity consistency and third-party evidence without manufacturing mentions.

## Entity consistency checklist

| Element | Where to verify |
|---|---|
| Official brand name | Homepage, About, Organization schema, social profiles |
| Product/category description | Homepage, product pages, docs, app stores, GitHub, directories |
| Founders/key experts | About page, author bios, LinkedIn, conference profiles |
| SameAs links | Organization/Person schema and visible footer/about page |
| Pricing/plan names | Pricing page, docs, support articles |
| Integrations | Integration pages, marketplace listings, docs |
| Local data | Google Business Profile, Bing Places, NAP consistency, local directories |

## Authentic third-party surfaces

Prioritize real, verifiable surfaces:

- industry directories and marketplaces;
- app stores and integration marketplaces;
- GitHub/GitLab package READMEs for developer tools;
- review platforms where customers genuinely review;
- community discussions where staff can answer transparently;
- case studies with permission;
- podcasts/webinars/conference pages;
- partner pages and integration docs;
- local business profiles and citations for local SEO.

## Do not do

- Do not buy fake mentions or comments.
- Do not create sockpuppet Reddit/forum posts.
- Do not create “best X” listicles that secretly rank the owner first without disclosure.
- Do not fabricate award pages, badges, customer logos, or review markup.
- Do not spam GitHub issues, forums, or communities.
